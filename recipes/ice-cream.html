Ice Cream

Description:

A cold and sweet treat for the whole family. Made with cream, milk, sugar and eggs

Ingredients:

  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Whole Milk
  • Sugar
  • Egg Yolks
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Simmer cream, milk, sugar and salt until sugar is completely disolved. Remove pot from heat.
  2. Wisk egg yolks together, then slowly wisk the cream mixture into the egg yolks.
  3. Return the mixture to the stovetop and cook unitl 170Â° F.
  4. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl.
  5. Chill overnight
  6. Churn in ice-cream machine.
