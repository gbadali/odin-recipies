Rice

Description:

Its just rice.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Rinse rice at least 3 times or until the water is clear.
  2. Place rice in saucepan and add 2 times as much water. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Place saucepan over medium heat until it starts to boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low and cover saucepan, cook for 20 minutes. Do not open cover.
  5. Remove from stove and serve immediately.
