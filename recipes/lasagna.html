Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layerd cake of pasta with delicious meat, tomato and cheese sauce between the layers. It is almost as delicious when bought from the store and cooked at home so I am not sure why you are cooking it from scratch.

Ingredients

Homemade Lasgana:

  • all purpose four
  • whole eggs
  • egg yolks
  • olive oil

Sauce

  • ground beef
  • ground pork
  • vegetable oil
  • yellow onion
  • garil
  • thyme
  • fennel seeds
  • salt
  • pepper
  • can crushed tomatoes
  • tomato paste

Assembly

  • ricotta cheese
  • egg
  • mozzerlla cheese
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the pasta.
  2. Make the sauce.
  3. Mix the cheeses with the egg.
  4. Cook the noodles.
  5. Add the sauce, then a layer of noodles then some of the cheese.
  6. Repeat the above until you run out of ingredents.
  7. Cover with foil and cook in 375Â° F oven for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for 25 minutes more.
  8. Cool for 15 minutes. Cut and serve.
